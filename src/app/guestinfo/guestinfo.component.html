McConnell AFB Free Open House Airshow

McConnell Air Force Base is opening its gates to the public during the Frontiers in Flight Open House and Airshow, September 8th and 9th! Come and see how Team McConnell contributes to the Air Capital of the World as the true Home of Air Refueling.

The base is open to the public on Saturday and Sunday from 9 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. The U.S. Air Force Thunderbirds are scheduled to perform alongside the U.S. Army Golden Knights Parachute Team and plenty of other professional airshow performers!

Admission is completely FREE to the public! We hope to see you here!

Permitted Items

  1. Diaper Bags for individuals with small children or special needs and small purses not to exceed 1 cubic foot
  2. Strollers, wheelchairs and electric scooters
  3. Cameras, and/or Camcorders (without bags)
  4. Lawn chairs

The following items are prohibited in the Open House area.

  1. Weapons: (i.e. guns, knives, pepper spray, batons, fireworks, etc.)
  2. Illegal Drugs, Outside Alcoholic beverages, and glass bottles
  3. Outside food and drink
  4. Pets (i.e. dogs, cats, hamsters, snakes, etc.) exception: service dogs
  5. Sports Equipment: (i.e. roller skates, bicycles, skateboards, in-line skates, etc.)
  6. UAV/Drones
  7. Large Hand carried Items: (i.e. tote bags, backpacks, coolers, etc.)
  8. People are subject to search.
